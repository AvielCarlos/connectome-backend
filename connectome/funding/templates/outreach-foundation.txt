**The Problem**

Despite unprecedented access to information and technology, rates of purposelessness, anxiety, and disconnection are at historic highs. The missing layer isn't more content — it's a system that genuinely learns who you are and actively guides you toward your own flourishing.

**What We're Building**

Connectome is a personalized AI brain called Ora that learns your goals, values, and engagement patterns, then generates adaptive daily experiences that move you measurably toward fulfilment. Unlike engagement-maximizing apps, we optimize for genuine wellbeing — tracked via a continuous, evolving fulfilment score.

Five specialized agents work in concert: Discovery, Coaching, Recommendation, UI Generator, and Feedback Analyst. The system runs a 50/50 explore/exploit feedback loop, always balancing expanding your horizon with reinforcing what works.

**Why Nonprofit**

When revenue comes from engagement, the incentive is to keep people scrolling — not flourishing. As a nonprofit (currently filing for 501(c)(3) and Canadian charitable status), we are structurally bound to serve user wellbeing. Revenue from a freemium model ($12.99/mo) and aggregate research data funds the mission, not returns.
